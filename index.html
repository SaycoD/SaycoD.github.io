About Me

My name is Marco Moutinho and I am a developer who is very passionate about software development, whether it be web, mobile, desktop, or backend. I enjoy writing software and increasing my knowledge in my spare time. I believe my passion is what drives me to always work at my best. This allows me to be very productive and keep focus.

I enjoy taking on challenging tasks and prefer them over simple ones. When working on projects, not only do I focus on getting the job done but I also place a high priority on code quality, readability, reusability, and robustness. I am a strong and committed team player, with excellent communication skills. I have also been able to master the ability to work independently and efficiently, and I consider myself to be a quick learner.

When not writing, or learning about, code, I enjoy gaming. I also do my best to stay healthy by eating right and going to the gym 4 to 5 times a week.
